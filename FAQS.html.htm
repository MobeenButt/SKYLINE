Airline FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is the baggage allowance?
The baggage allowance varies depending on the class of your ticket. Please check our website for detailed information.
Question 2: How can I book a ticket online?
You can easily book a ticket through our website. Visit the booking page, select your destination, and follow the instructions to complete the booking process.
Question 3: What in-flight services are available?
Our in-flight services include meals, entertainment, and comfortable seating. Premium classes may offer additional amenities.
Question 4: Can I bring my pet on board?
Yes, we allow pets on board. Please review our pet policy for specific guidelines and requirements.
Question 5: How can I change my flight reservation?
To change your flight reservation, log in to your account on our website and navigate to the "Manage Booking" section. Follow the prompts to make the desired changes.
Are there any travel restrictions due to COVID-19?
Yes, there may be travel restrictions in place. Check our website or contact our customer service for the latest information on COVID-19 travel guidelines.
Question 7: How can I request a refund for my ticket?
Refund requests can be submitted through our website or by contacting our customer service. Please review our refund policy for eligibility criteria.
Question 8: What types of payment do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and online payment systems. Check our booking page for the full list of accepted payment options.
Can I choose my seat when booking a ticket?
Yes, you can choose your seat during the booking process. Seat selection options may vary based on the ticket type and availability.
Question 10: Are there any discounts for children or senior citizens?
Yes, we offer discounts for children and senior citizens. Check our promotions or contact our customer service for information on available discounts.
